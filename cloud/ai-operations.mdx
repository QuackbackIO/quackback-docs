---
title: What counts as an AI operation?
description: Understanding the aiOpsPerMonth quota on Pro and Scale.
---

An **AI operation** is one chat-completion (LLM) call. Embeddings, vector
search, and full-text search do not count.

| Action | Cost |
|---|---|
| Generate a summary for one post | 1 |
| Assess one merge-suggestion candidate batch | 1 |
| Classify sentiment for one signal | 1 |
| Extract feedback from one external item (e.g. Intercom message, Zendesk ticket) | 1 |

## Excluded from the counter

- **Embeddings** — used for semantic search, merge prefilter, Help Center
  search. Cheap and pervasive; counting them would feel arbitrary.
- **Failed retries** — only the final successful attempt counts toward
  your quota.
- **Pure SQL operations** — full-text search, vector similarity search,
  analytics aggregations.

## Quotas by tier

| Tier | AI operations / month |
|---|---|
| Starter | None — AI features are not included on Starter |
| Pro | 2,500 |
| Scale | 25,000 |

If you exceed your quota, AI features pause until the start of the next
month, or you can upgrade for a higher quota.
