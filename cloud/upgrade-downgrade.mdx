description: How tier changes work, what happens when you hit a limit, and what survives a downgrade.

## Hitting a limit

When you try to do something that exceeds your tier's limit (e.g.
inviting a second team member on Starter), Quackback returns a clear
error message with the limit, your current usage, and a CTA to upgrade.
Existing data is unaffected — you can keep editing, voting on, and
commenting on what you have.

## Upgrading

Upgrades take effect immediately. New limits are pushed to your
workspace within a few seconds. We prorate the upgrade against your
current billing cycle.

## Downgrading

When you downgrade (e.g. Pro → Starter), **existing data stays
accessible**. Boards, posts, seats, integrations all keep working. You
just won't be able to create new ones above the new tier's limits until
you upgrade again.

We never delete your data on downgrade. The over-limit state itself
is the upgrade pressure if your team wants to add more.

### Examples

- **You have 8 boards on Pro and downgrade to Starter (1-board limit).**
  All 8 boards stay live. You can edit and use them. You cannot create
  new boards until you upgrade or delete enough boards to be under the
  cap.
- **You have 6 team seats on Pro and downgrade to Starter (1-seat limit).**
  All 6 admins/members keep access. You cannot invite new admins/members
  until you free up seats.
- **You're using Advanced AI on Pro and downgrade to Starter (Basic AI
  only).** Auto-summaries, duplicate suggestions, sentiment, and
  conversation extraction stop running on new posts. Existing
  AI-generated artifacts remain visible. Semantic search and similar-post
  suggestions keep working — those are Basic AI and available on every
  tier.

## Trials

Pro and Scale both include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required
to start. At the end of the trial, your workspace downgrades to Starter
unless you've added a payment method.
