# Welcome to Quackback

Quackback helps you collect user feedback, manage feature requests, and share your product roadmap.

## Quick Start

1. **Create a workspace** - Set up your first feedback board
2. **Embed the widget** - Add feedback collection to your app
3. **Manage feedback** - Review and organize user submissions
4. **Share your roadmap** - Keep users informed about what's next

## Features

- **Feedback Boards** - Collect and organize feature requests from your users
- **Public Roadmap** - Share your product direction and build transparency
- **Changelog** - Announce releases and keep users informed
