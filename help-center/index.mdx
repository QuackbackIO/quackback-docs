# Help Center

Give your users answers before they ask. The help center is a standalone knowledge base served on its own subdomain (or custom domain) where you can publish articles organized by category.

<Warning>
The help center is an experimental feature. Enable the `helpCenter` flag in **Admin > Settings > Experimental** to get started.
</Warning>

## Get started

- **[Set up your help center](/help-center/setup)** — Enable the feature, configure your subdomain, and choose access control
- **[Organize with categories](/help-center/categories)** — Create categories to group related articles
- **[Write and publish articles](/help-center/articles)** — Author, format, and publish help content

## Customize

- **[Use a custom domain](/help-center/custom-domains)** — Serve from `help.yourcompany.com` instead of the default subdomain
- **[Search and discovery](/help-center/search)** — How hybrid keyword + semantic search works
